Welcome to my webpage! My name is João Miguel Felício dos Reis. For stability and consistency, call me Joao Reis. I am a Marie-Curie Early Stage Research (ESR) fellow at the DeFI team of Inria-Saclay, as well as a PhD candidate at CMAP in Ecole Polythecnique. I am working in the area of Uncertainty Quantification (UQ).

Main Research:

My research focuses on domain decomposition techniques to solve stochastic PDEs. I am interested in efficient ways of estimating quantities of interest from the solutions of these models using Monte-Carlo methods. In particular, I am developing stochastic preconditioners that are adapted to each Monte-Carlo sample and can significantly accelerate the resolution of each sampled problem. This is a project under the UTOPIAE network UTOPIAE network, from which I am part of, too. My work is supervised by Pietro Congedo and Olivier Le Maître.
