ABOUT ME

Musicians, painters and dancers are all artists. And so are Mathematicians!

Welcome to my webpage! My name is João Miguel Felício dos Reis and I do Maths! For stability and consistency, call me Joao Reis. I am a Marie-Curie Early Stage Research (ESR) fellow at the DeFI team of Inria-Saclay, as well as a PhD candidate at CMAP in Ecole Polythecnique. I am working under the supervision of Pietro Congedo and Olivier Le Maître in the area of Uncertaity Quantification (UQ). In particular, I am working on domain decomposition techiques to solve large scale problems. The main applications are with respect to Aerospace Engineering. This is the main goal of the UTOPIAE network UTOPIAE network, from which I am part of, too. I am also part of the project MiND, a Machine Learning project for data classification in the same field of engeneering. You can drop me a message if you want to discuss about any of my projects or if you want to share something you think it's awesome. If you like photography, check out my Flirk account and let me know what you think, too.
